--

My name is [Name], a data engineering professional with a background in degree/field] from school]. I have experience working with 2–3 core tools/technologies, e.g. Python, SQL, Apache Spark, AWS, etc...], and I'm particularly interested in area of focus, e.g. pipeline development, data modeling, cloud infrastructure].

Most recently, I briefly describe your latest role, internship, or project — what you built, what problem it solved, and the impact/scale].

(Optionally) - Before that, I one sentence on a previous role or notable project if applicable]

I'm now looking to what you're seeking — e.g. grow my skills in a production environment, work on large-scale data systems, contribute to a data-driven team], and I'm excited about this opportunity because one genuine reason tied to the company or role].
